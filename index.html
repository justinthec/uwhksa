UWHKSA
滑大港生會

University of Waterloo Hong Kong Student Association (UWHKSA) was established in January 2013. As of September 2013, the UWHKSA has over 300 members, and this number is increasing. In the future, we will continue to expand and offer many more exciting events, to enrich our members' student career while at the University of Waterloo.

滑鐵盧大學香港學生會（UWHKSA）成立於2013年1月。截至2013年9月，本會會員人數已經超過三百名。隨着會員人數不斷增加，接下來定必不斷創新，及計劃跟其他學會合作，務求豐富會員們在滑鐵盧大學的學習生涯。
